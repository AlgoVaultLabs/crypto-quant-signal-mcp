Why integrate with AlgoVault?

  • ♦Composite verdict, not raw indicators. One JSON response replaces 26-indicator vote-counting in your agent code.
  • ♦Cross-venue intelligence. Funding spreads, regime, and sentiment fused across 5 exchanges — not derivable from any single-venue API.
  • ♦Merkle-verified. Every signal anchored to Base L2 via on-chain Merkle proof. 115,194+ calls, 45+ batches at last snapshot.
  • ♦Extensible by design. Adding a new exchange, MCP client, agent framework, or trading platform is an additive change — index pages auto-rebuild.
