◆ How It Works

1

Signal Created

Every BUY/SELL signal is hashed using keccak256(coin, signal, confidence, timeframe, timestamp, price) at the moment it's generated, BEFORE the outcome is known.

2

Hash Stored

The hash is stored alongside the signal in our database. The hash is deterministic and can be independently recomputed by anyone with the signal data.

3

Daily Batch

At 00:05 UTC, all new signal hashes are assembled into a Merkle tree. Each leaf is a signal hash; the tree produces a single root that commits to the entire set.

4

On-Chain Anchor

The Merkle root is published to a smart contract on Base L2 — permanent, immutable, publicly verifiable by anyone.

This means we cannot retroactively edit, delete, or fabricate signals. Every signal in our track record existed before the market moved. Verify any signal above, or inspect the contract directly on Basescan.
